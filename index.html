The King of Fighters: Battle de Paradise - Translation

Note: if you are just looking for the translation patch, go here.

This tool allows you to translate the Neo Geo Pocket Color exclusive game The King of Fighters: Battle de Paradise into English.
Anyone can contribute and improve the translation by editing texts and graphics directly in the browser, making this a collaborative translation project.

How to contribute

  1. Open the original King of Fighters, The - Battle de Paradise (Japan).ngc ROM at the form above
  2. Translate or improve any untranslated text you found in the game
  3. Click Save translated ROM and open the generated ROM in an emulator or on a flashcard
  4. Check that your new translated texts show correctly
  5. Click Export translations and follow the instructions on screen
